MOON

The Moon has no atmosphere, liquid water, or breathable air. However scientists believe future lunar bases could support human life using underground habitats, solar power, and water extracted from ice at the lunar poles.

MARS

Mars is the most promising planet for human colonization. It has seasons, polar ice caps, and a day length similar to Earth. Future settlers may live in pressurized domes and grow food using hydroponic farming systems.

UNIVERSE

The observable universe contains more than two trillion galaxies. Each galaxy contains billions of stars and planetary systems, making the universe unimaginably vast.
